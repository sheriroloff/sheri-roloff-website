About Sheri

Illustrator. Writer. Designer.

Sheri is an illustrator, writer and designer with a passion for storytelling. Whether it’s a quirky book project or a complex branding project, she brings a multifaceted creative approach to develop stories that connect with people on a visceral level. Sheri believes storytelling is more than a creative outlet – it’s what makes us human and helps us make sense of the world. And that’s why she's so passionate about connecting with people through the art of story.

Sheri's debut picture book “Hide & Go Seek-A-Saurus” won a 2019 Gold IBPA Benjamin Franklin Award and is available for sale in her store and on Amazon. Sheri is also an avid zine maker and released her zine compilation book “Mini Zine Magic” in 2020.

If you're interested in working with Sheri on a custom project or freelance opportunity, send an email to Roloff.Sheri@gmail.com. For the latest, follow Sheri on Instagram.
